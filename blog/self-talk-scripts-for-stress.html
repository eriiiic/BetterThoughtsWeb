Self‑Talk Scripts for Stressful Moments

January 26, 2025 · 4 min read
“This is hard, and I can do hard things.”
“My thoughts are loud, not necessarily true.”
“I’ll take the next kind action; it doesn’t have to be perfect.”
“Breathe out slowly; my body knows how to settle.”
“Future‑me will thank me for starting small right now.”
