Breaking Free from Common Anxiety Thought Patterns

February 1, 2025 · 6 min read
Catastrophizing: “This will ruin everything.” → “It’ll be uncomfortable, not ruinous.”
Intolerance of Uncertainty: “I must know now.” → “I can proceed with partial info.”
Probability Overestimation: “It’ll probably go wrong.” → “What’s the base rate? What’s within my control?”
