CBT Micro‑Skills You Can Practice Anywhere

January 29, 2025 · 4 min read
Exhale Longer: Inhale 4, exhale 6–8.
Name It: “I’m noticing anxiety,” not “I am anxiety.”
One Balanced Sentence: Write a believable alternative thought.
Tiny Value Action: Do a 60‑second kindness/courage task.
