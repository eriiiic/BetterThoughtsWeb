Growth Mindset vs. Fixed Mindset: A CBT View

January 25, 2025 · 4 min read
Fixed: “I’m just not good at this.”
Growth: “I’m learning skills with practice.”
Reframe with Evidence: List small improvements from recent efforts.
Run a Micro‑Experiment: Practice 10 minutes a day for one week and measure progress.
Celebrate Process: Reward showing up, not just results.
