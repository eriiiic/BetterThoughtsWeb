Cognitive Distortions: A Quick Cheatsheet

January 21, 2025 · 5 min read

All-or-Nothing

“If it’s not perfect, it’s worthless.” → “Progress counts.”

Catastrophizing

“It’ll be a disaster.” → “Challenging, not catastrophic.”

Mind Reading

“They think poorly of me.” → “I can’t know that.”

Overgeneralizing

“This always happens.” → “This happened once.”

Should Statements

“I should…” → “I prefer / I will.”

Emotional Reasoning

“I feel it, so it’s true.” → “Feelings aren’t facts.”

Keep this nearby when reframing thoughts in BetterThoughts.
