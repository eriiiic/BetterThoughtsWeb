Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01

BetterThoughts respects your privacy. We do not require an account and we do not collect personal information. The app stores content and preferences locally on your device using Apple’s Core Data.

  • Local storage only: Quotes, categories, bookmarks, and dismissed cards are saved on-device.
  • No login: The app works entirely without creating an account.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage metrics (e.g., screen opens, feature usage) to improve the experience. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Data sharing: We do not sell or share personal data.
  • Contact: Email contact@betterthoughts.app for privacy questions or requests.

If you uninstall the app, locally stored data is removed from your device.
